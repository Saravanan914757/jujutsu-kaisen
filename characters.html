Meet the Powerful Characters

Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is the most powerful sorcerer, possessing the Limitless Cursed Technique and the Six Eyes.

Ryomen Sukuna

Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses, is an ancient curse with immense power and overwhelming combat abilities.

Yuta Okkotsu

Yuta Okkotsu is a special-grade sorcerer with immense potential, being the wielder of Rika Orimoto's powerful cursed spirit.

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi Fushiguro, a skilled sorcerer, possesses the Ten Shadows Technique, allowing him to summon powerful shikigami.
